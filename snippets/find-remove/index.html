Recursive find and remove

2022-03-31
snippetos

Find all files/directories based on a name pattern and remove them recursively from a base directory.

For files:

find . -name "*.bak" -type f -exec rm {} \;

For directories:

find . -name "node_modules" -type d -exec rm -rf {} \;

Lets Write a Blog - Part 2: Zola

Zola (formally Gutenberg) is a SSG written in Rust. Zola has it's own templating engine Tera created by the same author and follows a similar design in it's templating to Jinja 2. Distributed as a single binary Zola has a much smaller footprint than the other SSGs we'll be looking at, it also promises to be fast (comparable to Hugo) and runs from a simple augmented markdown including shortcodes and custom internal linking.
